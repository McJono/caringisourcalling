Who We Are

Built by a support worker who was tired of broken promises.

Caring Is Our Calling was founded by someone who has worked inside the NDIS system — who has seen what happens when agencies grow too fast, stretch their teams too thin, and forget that every client is a person, not a number.

We exist to do things differently. By design, we will never have more than 8 clients at any one time. This isn't a compromise — it's the whole point.

That small number means every client gets a dedicated, consistent support team. The same familiar faces, week in and week out. No roster churn. No ghost workers. Just real, relationship-based support.

What We Do

Specialist behaviour support, from assessment to implementation.

Behaviour Support Plans

Comprehensive BSPs developed in collaboration with clients, families, and allied health professionals. We take the time to understand the whole person — not just the behaviour.

Behaviour Management

Individualised behaviour management strategies grounded in evidence-based practice. We focus on proactive approaches, skill-building, and environmental adjustments.

Support Coordination

Guidance navigating your NDIS plan, connecting you with the right services, and ensuring you get the most from your funded supports.

Allied Health Liaison

We work alongside psychologists, occupational therapists, and speech pathologists to ensure a cohesive, multidisciplinary approach to your support.

Daily Living Support

Personalised in-home and community support to build independence, life skills, and meaningful participation in everyday life.

Training & Advice

Training for support workers and families on behaviour management approaches, restrictive practices awareness, and positive behaviour support.
